# Personal Tips

- Be consistent - that’s the most important tip, no matter what your mood is, no matter how gloomy the day is, it doesn’t matter, be consistent
- Start small → increase study time / number of topics as you become more consistent, the key is to form a habit and then optimize certain process
- For folks who are already working - plan your day ahead - this way you can plan your study time, studying/practicing for 1-1.5 hours is enough, try to study before your work shift begins.
- For DSA - focus on patterns and practice as much as you can
- For freshers → forget AI, focus on fundamentals → DSA, DBMS, OS, Computer Networks, one language of your choice (do mastery in it, Java, Python or NodeJS). Once this much is done, learn frontend, then backend
- Don’t just cram things, have a recursive way of thinking and learning
- Use chatgpt a lot to study stuff rather going through endless youtube tutorials, chatgpt gives you everything you need in one place
